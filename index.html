Payment Allocation Priority
1st — Wiping → 2nd — Housekeeping → 3rd — Pickup
How It Works
1. When a waiter pays, the system calculates all missed days from their last payment (or join date).
2. Any previous pending amounts are carried over.
3. Any previous advance is added to the incoming payment.
4. Payment is applied in order: Wiping → Housekeeping → Pickup.
5. If payment exceeds total dues, the remainder becomes advance (credit for next payment).
6. Last paid date is automatically updated to today.
