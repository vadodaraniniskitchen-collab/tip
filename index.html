Payment Allocation Priority
1st — Wiping (full) → 2nd — Housekeeping (50%) + 2nd — Pickup (50%)
How It Works
1. When a waiter pays, the system calculates all missed days from their last payment (or join date).
2. Any previous pending amounts are carried over.
3. Any previous advance is added to the incoming payment.
4. Wiping is cleared first. The remaining amount is split 50/50 between Housekeeping and Pickup.
5. If one of HK or Pickup clears fully before using its half-share, the leftover flows to the other.
6. If payment exceeds total dues, the remainder becomes advance (credit for next payment).
7. Last paid date is automatically updated to today.
